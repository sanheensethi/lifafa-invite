Every concept below = one buildable invitation “world”. Each comes with a ready-to-paste Meta AI prompt to generate an animated GIF/loop background you can download.

How to generate the assets in Meta AI

1. Open Meta AI (in WhatsApp, Instagram, or the Meta AI app) and type “Imagine: <prompt>”.

2. When the image appears, tap Animate to turn it into a short looping motion clip, then download it (GIF/MP4).

3. Tips: keep motion subtle & seamless, prefer dark backgrounds (so gold text stays readable on top), and always add “seamless loop, no text”. We then layer your names/details over it in the template.

The engine: every template = Container metaphor × Art direction × Signature interaction × Motion personality. Swap any dial → a new world, same underlying toolkit.
